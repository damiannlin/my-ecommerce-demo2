Help Center (FAQ)

Q1: How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our product pages and click "Add to Cart." When you're ready, click the cart icon and follow the checkout steps, entering shipping & payment details.

Q2: What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Q3: Can I track my shipment?

Yes. After your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Use that on the carrier's website or our "Order Status" page to track progress.

Q4: How do I return or exchange an item?

Please see our Shipping & Returns page for full instructions on returns and exchanges.

Q5: How can I contact customer support?

You can email us at support@demostore.com or use the chat button at the bottom right to talk to our AI assistant.
